FAQ

Who is Deepsel?

Deepsel was founded in 2019 by Tim Tran - a software engineer in Hanoi, whose mission was to tap and grow the next generation of software developers and deliver excellence in software products. Initially building custom software for ecommerce businesses in Vietnam, Deepsel discovered the growing need, as well as the shortage and high costs, of software engineering in developed countries. Tim sought to connect these needs with the growing pool of highly skilled technical talents in Vietnam.

Are you an American company?

Yes, Deepsel Inc. is an American corporation incorporated in Delaware. Our primary development team is located in Hanoi, Vietnam.

How will I communicate with the team?

Our standard mode of communication is email or Slack, but you are free to choose the platform you want to talk on. You can also schedule video calls with our team, regardless of timezone differences, which we will accommodate.

What are your payment terms?

When billing for a fixed priced project, billing will be divided into milestones based on project completion. Some projects may require a deposit. If billing by the hour, we invoice weekly. Payment is due within 3 business days. All payments are invoiced in US dollars through Stripe, a trusted American payment processor.

Will you sign my NDA?

We can not sign an NDA before we have an initial description of your project and a budget figure to work with. We need to know if the requirements and client's expectations are a good fit to work with Deepsel, and an NDA can only be signed if it's a mutual agreement.

Can Deepsel take over my existing custom software?

Yes we can. We'll need a live demo of the software and access to audit your code. Once that's done, we'll send you a report that includes an analysis, recommendations and cost estimates for the takeover.

Who owns the code after it's done?

You do. Before we begin, we’ll send over a software development agreement in place that will assign all ownership of all the work and assets to you, as long as we’re paid for the services we perform.

How much will my project cost?

This can only be answered after the requirements collection process. After learning about the problem you're trying to solve, your budget, users, market, ...etc. we can then propose a solution that solves the problem while staying in your budget. This is the software consulting part of the project, and we give 4 free consulting hours per project. The result will be a proposal with a cost estimation.

Will you host my project after completion?

Yes, we sure can. We can manage cloud servers all over the world through AWS, Google Cloud etc., ask us about our hosting packages.

Do you write apps for iPhone and Android?

Yes, we do.
